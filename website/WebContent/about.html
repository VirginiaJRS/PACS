    Dr. Ziheng Sun is a research assistant profession in Cener for Spatial Information Science and Systems, George Mason University. His research interests include parameterless automatic classification of remote sensing images, especially very high resolution images. He also studies the theory about geoprocessing Web services and workflow and conducted a lot of experiments on them since 2009. His achievements have been published on IEEE Transactions on Geosicence and Remote Sensing, IEEE Journal of Selected Topics in Applied Earth Observations and Remote Sensing, Computers & Geosciences and Transactions in GIS.
